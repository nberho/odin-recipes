Chaparritas Recipe

Description

Here's a recipe for making super spicy chaparritas. You grab some tortillas and put a sausage on them. Then add some cheese. Before eating them, roll them like a fat cigar. Then eat them.

Ingredientes

  • Tortillas
  • Sausage
  • Cheese

Steps

  • Grab the tortilla
  • Open it
  • Put the sausage inside it
  • Add cheese on top
  • Roll it like a super huge cigar
  • Eat it
